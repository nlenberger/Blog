BIO311 Lab Report Gram and Acid Fast Staining

Oct 3, 2017 #Gram Staining  #Acid Fast Staining 
Introduction In this lab, two types of differential staining were performed. The first was Gram staining and the second was Acid Fast. Gram staining is one of the most commonly used staining procedures used in microbiology. Gram staining can characterize bacteria into two groups: gram positive bacteria or gram negative bacteria.
